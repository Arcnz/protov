Vivid Esports

The Introduction

The 3 leaders of Vivid met in a clan called Zync in the summer of 2016. Realizing that there was more competitive potential, Brovck, Raven and Zookuzero decided to create Vivid. Vivid sprouted very quickly and soon had over 60 community members and 4 competitive teams. As Vivid grew so did our goals and here we are today.
